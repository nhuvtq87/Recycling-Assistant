Recycling is not the solution to all our environmental problems, but it's a step in the right direction. **Recycling Assistant** is a mobile app designed to make recycling smarter—together.

## 💡 The Idea

We have developed an accurate, user-friendly, and educational mobile application that simplifies daily waste sorting. The core concept leverages modern technology to foster responsible consumption habits (SDG 12).

## 🚀 Key Features

* **Scans Waste via Camera:** Utilize your phone's camera to instantly identify waste items.
* **AI Classification:** The app automatically classifies scanned items as **Recyclable**, **Compostable**, or **Trash**.
* **Location-Based Logic:** Disposal guidance is customized by **ZIP code** and **local city rules**.
    * *Example:* A Starbucks cup can only be recycled in San Francisco; our app knows that.
* **Real-time Chatbot Assistance:** Instant, educational feedback and guidance on correct bin use.
* **Prevent "Wishcycling":** We reduce incorrect recycling by ensuring you sort correctly, decreasing landfill contributions.

## 🛠 Technology Stack

Our implementation feasibility is anchored in powerful, established AI technologies:

| Technology | Role | Function |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Watson Visual Recognition** | Computer Vision | Accurately identifies the waste item type. |
| **Watson Assistant Chatbot** | Conversational AI | Provides immediate recycling guidance and answers. |
| **Watson Natural Language Understanding** | Language Modeling | Handles complex user queries and follow-up questions. |
| **City Rules Database** | Location Services | Powers the local recycling logic and restrictions. |

## 🛡 Ethical Considerations

We are committed to building responsible technology:

* **Privacy First:** No personal data or face-tracking is collected.
* **Accuracy:** We use verified government sources for all recycling data.
* **Transparency:** The app explains the AI decision-making process to the user.
* **User Control:** Users can report misclassifications for continuous system improvement.

## 🏗 Conclusion

Recycling Assistant empowers users to make smarter recycling choices, encourages daily sustainable habits, and supports SDG 12 to drive responsible consumption. Let's make recycling smarter—together.
